What is fake news?

Many things you read online, particularly in your social media feeds, may appear to be real, but they are frequently not. False information is news, tales, or hoaxes that are intended to deceive or misinform readers. These articles are usually made to either influence people's opinions, push a political agenda, or create confusion, and they may be a lucrative business for internet publishers. False information can fool consumers by imitating recognised websites or utilising names and web addresses that are similar to those of respected news organisations.
