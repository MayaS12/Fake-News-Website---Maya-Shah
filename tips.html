Tips to avoid Fake News!

Tips and tricks to spot fake news:

  • Keep a skeptical mindset and ask yourself questions like: "Why has this story been written? Is it to persuade me of a certain viewpoint? Is it selling me a particular product? Or is it trying to get me to click through to another website? Am I being triggered?
  • Check the source. If it ends in .com or .co.uk or .gov or .edu, you probably have a credible website. A trusted web source like Snopes can also help you to identify fake news.
  • See who else is reporting on this story and if they are a trusted source.

  • Keep in mind what your biases are and what you want to believe.
  • Switch your search engines to increase your privacy and not be influenced by Google user data. A reccomended search engine is Duckduckgo
